How to use this app

Complete guide for kids and parents

Add to your iPhone home screen

1
Open the app URL in Safari (not Chrome)
2
Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up)
3
Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
4
Type a name like Daily Checklist and tap Add
The app will now appear as an icon on your home screen and open full screen like a regular app.

Add to Android home screen

1
Open the app URL in Chrome on your Android device
2
Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner
3
Tap Add to Home screen
4
Tap Add to confirm

Switching between kids

1
Tap Son or Daughter at the top to switch profiles
2
Each child has their own separate tasks, timers, and progress - switching does not affect the other child
Each child can have their own banner color. Tap the colored circle button (top right) to change it.

Morning, After School, and Night checklists

1
Tap Morning, After School, or Night to switch checklists
2
Each checklist has its own set of phases and tasks
3
The colored dots at the top show how many phases are complete in the current checklist

Using timers

1
Tap a phase to expand it, then tap GO to start the countdown timer
2
The timer turns amber when half the time is gone and red in the last minute
3
Tap II to pause the timer and RST to reset it back to full time
4
An alarm sound plays when the timer reaches zero
You can turn the alarm on or off using the toggle at the top of the page.

Checking off tasks

1
Tap the checkbox next to a task to mark it done - it will get a strikethrough
2
The progress bar at the top of each phase fills as you check off tasks
3
When all tasks in a phase are done, tap Mark phase as done to turn the card green

Repeating tasks (like retainer adjustment)

1
Some tasks repeat every few days. They show a blue badge with the schedule
2
When a task is due today, the row is highlighted yellow and the badge turns amber
3
When it is not due, the task is dimmed and cannot be checked - the badge shows days until next due
4
To change how often a task repeats, hover over the task and tap the Edit button next to the badge
Checking off a repeating task records today's date and automatically calculates the next due date.

Adding custom tasks

1
Expand a phase and type a task name in the Add a subtask box
2
To make it a repeating task, tap + Repeat and enter how many days between each occurrence
3
Tap + Add or press Enter to save the task
Custom tasks can be deleted by hovering over them and tapping the X button. Built-in tasks cannot be deleted but can be unchecked.

Reordering tasks

1
On a phone: press and slowly drag a task up or down using the three-line handle on the left
2
On a computer: click and drag the handle to reorder
The new order is saved automatically and will be remembered next time.

Saving the daily log

1
At the end of the checklist, tap Save today to log
2
This saves the results locally on this device AND sends a summary to Google Sheets
3
Parents can open the Google Sheet from any device to review daily progress for both kids
Save separately for each checklist (Morning, After School, Night) to get a complete picture of the day.

Changing the banner color

1
Tap the colored circle button in the top right corner
2
Tap any color in the grid to preview it
3
Tap Save color to apply it
Each child's color is saved separately and remembered on this device.

Resetting a checklist

1
Tap Reset at the bottom of the page
2
Confirm the reset - this clears all checkboxes and timers for the current child and checklist only
Resetting does not affect the other child or the other checklists (Morning / After School / Night).

Choose a banner color

How to use this checklist

Profiles

Tap Son or Daughter at the top to switch between your children. Each child has their own separate checklists, timers, and progress.

Checklists

There are three checklists per child: Morning, After School, and Night. Tap the tabs to switch between them.

Timers

Tap GO to start a phase timer. It will turn amber with 1 minute left and red when time is up. An alarm will sound when the timer finishes. Tap II to pause, RST to reset.

Checking off tasks

Tap the checkbox next to each task to mark it done. The progress bar fills as you complete tasks in a phase. Tap Mark phase as done when you finish a phase.

Repeating tasks

Some tasks (like adjusting retainers) repeat every few days. They show a blue badge with the repeat schedule. When it is not due today, the task is dimmed. When it is due, it is highlighted in yellow.

Adding tasks

Type a task in the Add a subtask box at the bottom of any phase and tap + Add or press Enter. You can also drag the three-line handle to reorder tasks.

Color theme

Tap the colored circle button (top right) to change the banner color. Each child can have their own color. The color is saved automatically.

Saving the log

Tap Save today to log to record the day. This saves locally on this device and also sends a summary to Google Sheets so parents can review progress from any device.

Resetting

Tap Reset to clear today's checkboxes and timers for the current child and checklist. It will ask you to confirm first.
